## Full Description

### Find Your People
frnds is where you meet new people who actually get you. Swipe through profiles, match based on shared vibes, and start conversations that matter.

### How It Works
- **Discover** — Swipe through profiles near you. Like someone? Swipe right. Not feeling it? Swipe left. It's that simple.
- **Match** — When you both swipe right, it's a match! Start chatting instantly.
- **Chat** — Send messages, snaps, and GIFs in real-time. Your conversations, your way.
- **Stories** — Share what you're up to with 24-hour stories. See what your frnds are doing.
- **Vibes** — Tag your interests and match with people who share them. Music, gaming, art, sports — find your tribe.

### Why frnds?
- Clean, modern design that feels good to use
- No fake profiles — real people, real connections
- Share moments through stories and snaps
- Dark mode by default (because we get it)
- Your privacy matters — control who sees what

### Features
- Tinder-style swipe to discover new people
- Real-time messaging with typing indicators
- Snapchat-style ephemeral stories
- Interest-based matching
- Online status and last seen
- Camera integration for snaps
- Profile customization with vibes/interests
- Push notifications for matches and messages
- Dark and light theme support
